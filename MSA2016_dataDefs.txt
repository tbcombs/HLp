

MSA2016 data definitions

NAME: Name of MSA

chPov_pc: Children living at or below poverty level (%)

hhsz: Average household size (# of people)

gini: Gini index of income inequality (higher = more inequality)

mhhi10k: Median household income (in $10,000s)

big: 0 = < 1 million people; 1 = 1 million or more people in MSA

unemp_pc: Unemployment rate (% of labor force)

rd: Index of racial & ethnic diversity; the probability that two people chosen at random will be from different races or ethnicities
